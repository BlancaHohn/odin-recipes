Royal Kaiserschmarrn



Kaiserschmarrn?


Kaiserschmarrn has the royalty already in its name and is truly a dessert for emperors and empresses.

Ingredients


  • 200 grams of flour
  • 30 grams of sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 40 g butter (for the pan)
  • a handful of raisins
  • 1 pinch icing sugar for sprinkling

The Steps


  1. For this delicious Kaiserschmarrn, first mix the flour, sugar, salt and yolk with the milk in a bowl to form a smooth, viscous batter.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff and then fold into the thick batter.
  3. Froth the butter in a large, flat pan so that it is very hot, pour in the batter and use a spatula to bake on both sides.
  4. Then bake the pan in a preheated oven at moderate heat (hot air approx. 180Â°C) for 10-12 minutes - until the Kaiserschmarren is lightly golden brown.
  5. Then remove the pan from the oven and tear the finished dough into irregular pieces with two forks.
  6. Now add the raisins, stir well and put the pan back into the hot oven for about 1 minute.
  7. Arrange the Schmarren on plates, sprinkle with sugar and serve with any compote or apple sauce.
  8. Enjoy!
