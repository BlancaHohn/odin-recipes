Delicious Schnitzel recipe



Schnitzel?


Schnitzel is a typical austrian dish, served with a slice of lemon and oftentimes with a potato salad.

Ingredients


  • 4 veal escalopes (160 g each)
  • 150 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • lemon
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 300 grams of breadcrumbs
  • 1 pack of shortening, oil or clarified butter

The Steps


  1. Gently pound the schnitzel between cling film. Salt the meat on both sides, dredge in flour, tap off, dip in the eggs and dredge in the breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry the schnitzel about 2 fingers high in shortening until golden. Shake the pan a little while baking so that the cutlets are evenly golden brown. Lift out the schnitzel and drain on kitchen paper.
  3. Cut the lemon into wedges and garnish the finished Wiener Schnitzel with lemon wedges.
  4. Enjoy!
