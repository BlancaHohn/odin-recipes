Delicious Schnitzel recipe


Schnitzel?



Schnitzel is a typical austrian dish, served with a slice of lemon and oftentimes with a potato salad.

Ingredients


  • tender chicken filet
  • Mehl
  • eggs
  • lemon
  • cooking oil

The Steps


  1. hammer the chicken flat
  2. put eggs-insides in one dish and the mehl in another
  3. let the flat chicken filet bathe in the egg-insides and afterwards in the mehl
  4. put cooking oil in pan and heat that mf chicken up until it's golden brown
  5. enjoy!
